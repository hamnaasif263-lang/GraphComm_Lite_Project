PROJECT OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This analysis integrates single-cell transcriptomics with clinical outcome prediction
to identify dormant tumor cell populations and stratify patients by relapse risk.

ANALYSIS PIPELINE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 1: Single-cell preprocessing and dormancy signature extraction
Stage 2: Patient-level feature aggregation and risk modeling
Stage 3: Window of risk estimation (6mo, 12mo, 24mo)
Stage 4: Professional visualization for publication
